The People Behind the Flowers

Thirty years in the making.
Still growing.

Michelle Damron

Floral Designer · UK-trained

From Harrods, London to Columbus

Michelle trained at floral college in the English Midlands and began her career at the famous flower halls of Harrods in London. She brought a European eye for garden design to Columbus.

"She learned to see in the gardens of the English Midlands."

Robb Damron

30 Years Floral Wholesale

From the South Side of Columbus

A Capital University art graduate, Robb spent thirty years in floral wholesale — learning every step from farm to retail. He knows what the earth gives, when, and from where.

"They met in the floral business in Columbus, and the idea for Lēf was already growing. That was over thirty years ago."
